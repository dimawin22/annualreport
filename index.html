Key Milestones of 2025

01. Vendor Team Transformation

groups
  • The Vendor team structure changed significantly.
  • Team size decreased, but efficiency, accountability, and ownership increased.
  • Stronger team collaboration emerged – not just inside Vendor, but across Localization and Sales.
  • Clear roles, clearer expectations, less chaos.

"This was not a 'cost-cutting' change – it was a quality upgrade."

03. Monthly Alpha Review

calculate

Another important operational milestone was launching the Monthly Alpha Calculation Review.

  • Over several months, we reviewed and fixed hundreds of calculation errors
  • These were cases where profit was silently lost due to incorrect alpha cloning, outdated task setups, and human copy-paste errors

Alphas = tasks in our system. Fixing this process directly stopped margin leakage and increased trust in our numbers.

04. Hunt&Hire Tool

Results

08. JPY Payments Optimization

Another important financial milestone.

  • Part of linguists were switched to JPY payments
  • This allowed us to use existing company JPY reserves more efficiently
  • Reduced currency pressure and improved cash-flow flexibility

Small change – real financial impact.
